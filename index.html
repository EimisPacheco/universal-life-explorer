Learn Mode — grab your own organs

Turn on camera
Click Motion Capture in the sidebar, then stand back so your shoulders and hips are visible.
Grab an organ
Put your hand over that spot on your own body and close your fist. The organ pops out and is explained aloud and on screen.
Put it back
Open your hand, or move it back against your body.
Ask a question
While holding an organ, hold T (or click the 🎤) and speak. Release to send.

Quiz Mode

How to play
The app names an organ — find it on your own body and make a fist there. Scores accuracy and streak.
Wrong answer
Shows what you actually grabbed and reveals the one you were looking for.
Esc
End the quiz
